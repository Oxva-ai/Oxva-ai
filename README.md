## What I'm Building

### [EasyEarns](https://easyearns.com) — The UK's Referral Marketplace

A moderated platform where people share referral rewards transparently.

Referral codes are usually buried across Reddit threads, forums, and random social posts — often outdated, low-trust, or impossible to compare.

EasyEarns gives them a proper home.

Every submission is reviewed before going live.  
Users can browse verified offers, compare rewards, and surface the deals that actually work.

---

## Platform at a Glance

| Surface | What it does |
|--------|-------------|
| **Referral Marketplace** | Browse, search, and filter deals by brand, category, reward type, and requirements |
| **Submit Flow** | Multi-step submission with live preview, brand detection, logo fetching, and screenshots |
| **Moderation Queue** | Approve, reject, bulk actions, and full audit trail |
| **Live Support Chat** | Real-time support with queue handling and session claiming |
| **Reputation & Leaderboard** | Community voting, activity scoring, and top contributors |
| **Admin Dashboard** | User management, moderation, analytics, brand controls, and site settings |
| **Blog & Articles** | SEO-friendly guides and markdown-rendered content |
| **Cookie Consent** | GDPR/CCPA-compliant consent management |

## What I Actually Ship

EasyEarns is not a demo project — it is a production platform with real moderation workflows, real users, and live operational tooling.

- **Production-first engineering** — loading, empty, error, and edge cases handled throughout
- **Mobile-first UX** — responsive layouts, accessible contrast, keyboard-friendly navigation
- **Security by default** — input validation, URL safety checks, IP bans, user bans, and audit logging
- **Performance-conscious architecture** — server rendering, cached queries, and lean bundles
